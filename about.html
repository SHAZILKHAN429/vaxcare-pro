Our Story

Born from a Vision for Accessible Healthcare

VaxCare Pro was founded in 2012 by a team of doctors and technology experts who believed that no one should struggle to access vaccination services. What started as a small local clinic network has grown into a nationwide platform trusted by over 50,000 patients.

Today, we partner with 120+ certified clinics, hospitals, and healthcare centers to provide seamless vaccination booking, digital health records, and patient education — all in one place.

  • Government-certified vaccination centers
  • Licensed and experienced healthcare professionals
  • WHO and CDC approved vaccines only
  • 24/7 patient support and helpline
  • Secure digital health records and certificates
