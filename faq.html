General Questions

6 Questions

What is VaxCare Pro?

VaxCare Pro is a comprehensive vaccination booking and healthcare platform that connects individuals with certified healthcare professionals for safe, convenient, and affordable vaccination services. We operate 15+ clinic locations across the country and offer home visit services.

Is VaxCare Pro certified by health authorities?

Yes. VaxCare Pro is fully certified by the CDC, FDA, and accredited by the National Healthcare Association. All our medical staff hold valid licenses and our clinics meet the highest international standards for vaccine administration.

What age groups do you serve?

We serve all age groups including:
  • Infants (0-12 months) — pediatric vaccination programs
  • Children (1-12 years) — childhood immunization schedule
  • Teenagers (13-17 years) — adolescent boosters
  • Adults (18-60 years) — adult vaccinations and travel vaccines
  • Seniors (60+ years) — flu, pneumonia, and shingles vaccines

Do you offer home vaccination services?

Yes! Our certified nurses can visit your home, office, or any convenient location. Home visit services are available for individuals and group bookings (families, corporate teams). A small travel fee may apply depending on location.

What documents do I need to bring?

Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID, your vaccination records (if available), insurance card (if applicable), and your booking confirmation. For children, a parent or guardian must be present with the child's birth certificate.

How do I get my vaccination certificate?

Vaccination certificates are issued digitally via email within 30 minutes of completing your vaccination. Physical copies are available upon request. Our digital certificates are internationally recognized and WHO compliant.

Booking Questions

5 Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Booking is easy! You can book online through our Services page, call our hotline at +1 (800) 829-2273, use our mobile app, or walk in to any of our 15+ clinic locations. Online bookings receive instant confirmation.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes. Appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 4 hours before the scheduled time with no penalty. Cancellations within 4 hours may incur a small fee. You can manage your appointments via our website, app, or by calling us directly.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your appointment to complete any required paperwork and pre-vaccination health screening. First-time patients should arrive 20 minutes early.

Can I book for multiple family members at once?

Absolutely! Our Family Booking feature allows you to schedule appointments for your entire family in one session. You can select different vaccines and times for each family member. Family bundles of 3 or more also qualify for a 15% group discount.

Is there a waiting time at the clinic?

Pre-booked appointments have an average wait time of under 5 minutes. Walk-in patients may wait 15-30 minutes depending on clinic volume. After vaccination, we ask all patients to remain for a 15-minute observation period as standard safety protocol.

Vaccine Questions

5 Questions

Which vaccines do you offer?

We offer over 40 vaccines including:
  • COVID-19 (primary + boosters)
  • Influenza (seasonal flu)
  • Hepatitis A and B
  • MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
  • Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis (Tdap)
  • Travel vaccines (Typhoid, Yellow Fever, Meningitis)
  • HPV, Varicella, Pneumococcal, and more

Are your vaccines FDA approved?

Yes. Every vaccine we administer is FDA-approved, WHO pre-qualified, and stored in certified cold-chain conditions. We source all vaccines directly from licensed manufacturers and run daily temperature monitoring at all storage facilities.

How do I know which vaccines I need?

Our free online Vaccine Assessment Tool helps you determine which vaccines are recommended based on your age, health status, travel plans, and vaccination history. You can also speak to one of our vaccine counselors for personalized guidance.

Can I receive multiple vaccines on the same day?

In most cases, yes. Many vaccines can be safely administered on the same day. However, certain combinations require spacing for optimal effectiveness. Our medical staff will review your records and advise you on the safest schedule.

Do vaccines require a prescription?

Most vaccines at VaxCare Pro do not require a prior prescription. A brief health assessment is completed at the time of your visit by our licensed nurse. Certain specialized vaccines may require a doctor's referral.

Safety Questions

4 Questions

What side effects should I expect?

Common mild side effects include soreness at the injection site, mild fever, fatigue, or headache — all normal signs your immune system is responding. These typically resolve within 1-2 days. Severe reactions are extremely rare. Our staff will brief you on all specific side effects before each vaccination.

What if I have allergies?

Always disclose all known allergies to our healthcare staff before vaccination. We have emergency protocols and epinephrine on-site for any allergic reactions. Patients with severe allergy histories will be monitored for 30 minutes post-vaccination.

Are vaccines safe during pregnancy?

Certain vaccines are specifically recommended during pregnancy (like influenza and Tdap) for the protection of both mother and baby. Others should be avoided. Our team will review your pregnancy stage and advise the safest plan according to current CDC guidelines.

What hygiene protocols do your clinics follow?

All our clinics follow WHO-level hygiene standards including single-use sterile needles, PPE for all staff, hospital-grade disinfectants, HEPA-filtered air systems, and regular third-party audits. All surfaces are sanitized between every patient.
